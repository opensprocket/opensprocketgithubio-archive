Cloudi

What is Cloudi?

Cloudi will be a weather forecast site, which will provide accurate weather statistics according to your current location. By using clean design, information will be conveyed clearly and quickly. Upon visiting the site, the visitor will know about the weather for that day.

As of the time of writing this, www.cloudi.info is available for purchase.

The .info Top-level Domain (TLD) will be used because it is an information driven website.

Logo

This logo uses simple shapes and colors to convey its purpose and was created specifically for this project. Since the logo was built as a scalable vector graphic (SVG), it will be able to scale to all screen sizes and shapes.
