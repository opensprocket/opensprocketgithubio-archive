Amenities

The following amenities are offered to guests at no additional cost or surcharge:

  • Free Continental Breakfast (Served 5:30am to 10:30am)
  • Exercise room
  • Indoor heated pool
  • High speed Wi-fi internet access
  • Temple shuttle
  • Free Laundry
  • Playroom for children

Other amenities that are offered as low-cost services:

  • Room Service
  • Babysitting services
  • Computer lab which has been equipped for family history work.

If there is something that you don't see here that you need, please ask our conciergerie or contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Reception Services

We offer a wide range of wedding services at our exciting venues. Services include catering, photography, venue services (tear down and setup), entertainment and many more.

Reception garden venue

Take advantage of our world-class chef and catering staff and have your reception meals catered. All meals are prepared and served right from the venue, so you'll never have to worry about the food being late!

We have menu's for full meals, appetizers and desserts only. In addition to that, we can serve a custom menu to accommodate for food allergies and dietary preferences/restrictions.

All services are offered at a competitive and affordable rate.

Please contact us for up to date catering information, venue availability and any other questions regarding reception services.
