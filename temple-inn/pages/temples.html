Provo, Utah Temple

History

The Provo Utah temple was announced on August 14th, 1967 and the groundbreaking for the temple was on June 15th, 1969. The temple was dedicated three years later on, February 9th, 1972. The Ogden Utah temple was visually similar until it was rebuilt.
