Provo Utah Temple

History

The Provo Utah temple was announced on August 14th, 1967 and the groundbreaking for the temple was on June 15th, 1969. The temple was dedicated three years later on, February 9th, 1972. The Ogden Utah temple was visually similar until it was rebuilt.

Atlanta Georgia Temple

History

The Atlanta Georgia temple was announced on April 2nd, 1980. Ground was broken on March 7th, 1981 and dedicated on June 1st, 1983. The temple was then rededicated on November 14th, 1997. The temple was rededicated (making this the third dedication) on May 1st, 2011.
