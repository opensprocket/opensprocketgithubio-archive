Destination Spotlight

Atlanta Georgia Temple

Did you know that the Atlanta Georgia temple was the first temple dedicated by President Gordon B. Hinckley? He would go on to dedicate or rededicate 89 other temples.

The original plans for the temple did not have an Angel Moroni on the spire. It was later added after a Journalist for the Atlanta Journal-Constitution commented on the lack of a spire. A short time later, the Church architect revised the plans and included it.
