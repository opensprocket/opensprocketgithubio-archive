Services

Temple Inn & Suites is proud to offer a full range of specialized services to guests. This is done in order to make your stay here as relaxing and easy as possible. To add a given service, please make a reservation and make a note of which service you would like.

Once we recieve your request, a representative from Temple Inn & Suites will reach out to you by email for confirmation and additional details.

Some of our specialized services are as follows, please see the services page for more information

  • Playroom with budget friendly babysitting services during normal hours
  • Long-term accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time missionaries
  • A Church history themed reception & sitting area
  • Full service catering with a simple menu for wedding receptions (Please reserve in advance!)
  • Library which supports family history research
