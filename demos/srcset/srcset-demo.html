How it Works

Here we have an image that is responsive to the width of the viewport. By using the img tag with the srcset and sizes attributes, the image below will only download and display the size appropriate to the viewport.

Open up your browser inspection tool (Ctrl+Shift+I), turn on your mobile emulator and pick a device.

Then switch to the network tab and reload the page. You should see the image closest to that resolution and pixel density being downloaded.
